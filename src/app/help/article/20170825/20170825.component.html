How to reset your password

When logged out

1. Open the Sign in / Sign up dialog by clicking the link at the top right of any page.

2. At the bottom of the dialog window, click "Sign in with email" then "Forgot password" to display the password reset screen.

3. Enter your email address and click "send" and a password reset link with the appropriate credentials attached to it will be sent to the given email address.

When logged in

1. Open the general settings page and scroll to the bottom to find the password update form.

2. Enter the required information and click "send" to reset your password. If you can't recall your original password, or you login through Facebook, Twitter or Google, it is possible to set a new password for your account by clicking "I forgot my password" and sending yourself a password reset link.

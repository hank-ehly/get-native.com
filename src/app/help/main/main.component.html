Frequently Asked Questions
  • 01 Is getnative free?

    Yes! getnative is a free online language learning resource.

  • 02 How do I begin a study session?

    Save a video to your dashboard by clicking the "save" button at the top of a video page. In your dashboard, click the "begin" button on the video to begin a study session.

  • 03 Who created this website?

    Hank Ehly had the initial idea and wrote the code. Michele Angeloro created the design.
